Sometimes you want to make a few words stand out within a block of text. We can achieve this by nesting tags inside a p tag. Links are often embedded within larger sections of text, and we use the a tag to mark them. For example, here is the course website as a link.

Lists

Lists in HTML are NESTED structures. First, you pick the list type (ul or ol), then you add list items (li) inside it. Lists can be UNORDERED (ul) or ORDERED (ol).
